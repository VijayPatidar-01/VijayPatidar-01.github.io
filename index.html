About Me

Turning Raw Rasters Into Real Insights

I'm a GeoAI & Remote Sensing Engineer from Madhya Pradesh, India — working at the intersection of satellite imagery, machine learning, and geospatial analysis.

My work involves building end-to-end systems that process raw satellite data — from SAR backscatter to multi-layer soil moisture maps — using platforms like Google Earth Engine alongside Python-based ML pipelines.

I'm passionate about solving real-world problems in agriculture and environmental monitoring. My projects span Sentinel-1, LISS-4, SMAP, and IMD climate grids — fused with machine learning to produce actionable geospatial outputs.

Every project I build is end-to-end — from raw data ingestion to frontend visualization — because I believe the best geospatial tools put insights directly in the hands of decision-makers.
